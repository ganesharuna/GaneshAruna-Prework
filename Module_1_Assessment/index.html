Hi! My name is Ganesh Aruna. I graduated from Vanderbilt University in May of 2020 with a degree in Biomedical Engineering and a minor in Engineering Management. My hobbies include reading, listening to podcasts, and playing ultimate frisbee.

Currently, the podcast I am binging is the history of Rome by Mike Duncan. I would highly recommend this podcasts to anyone, not to just those with an interest in history. Although there are a lot of unfamiliar names and places thrown around, he really blends together the content and narrates the information in a really captivating way. The podcast covers from the earliest days of Rome to the height of the empire under Augustus to the fall of the Western Empire. If this piqued your interest, I would also recommend listening to his other series, Revolutions.
